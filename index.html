Quit Synklok

Pro Tools jumps from 48 to 96 kHz. Your MADIface follows. Your M-1620 doesn't.

Now you've got dropouts, clicks, or audio at the wrong pitch — until you click through the web UI or the rack unit's front panel.

Synklok closes the loop. When the MADI input rate changes, it tells the M-1620 to match — within two seconds. You don't have to think about it.

Built to disappear

Three things matter. Synklok does them well, and nothing else.

Set and forget

Runs quietly in the menu bar. Launches at login. Once installed, you never have to think about it again.

Glance-able status

Connection state, current rate, MADI sync, and recent changes — all visible in one click. No hunting.

Built for pro audio

Native macOS app. Open source. No analytics. No accounts. Talks directly to the M-1620's REST API.

How it works

A simple loop, polling twice a second.
